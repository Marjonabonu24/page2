    About EKVATORIAL GROUP

    üè¨

    EKVATORIAL GROUP manufactures its products in strict accordance with the technical specifications provided by clients. Depending on the type of operation performed during garment production, various types of sewing equipment are utilized.

    The experimental workshop is equipped with a computer-aided design (CAD) system, and an automated pattern output system is also in use. This allows all sewing factories to receive original pattern layouts, ensuring high and consistent quality in finished garments.

    All production processes incorporate the latest technologies, and the company continues to expand each year in line with its dynamic growth.

    The materials used in the tailoring of uniform and departmental outerwear undergo rigorous multi-level testing in specialized laboratories and meet strict standard requirements.
